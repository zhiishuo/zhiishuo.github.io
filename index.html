About

I work at the intersection of multimodal learning, affective intelligence, and reliable AI system building. My research asks how language, vision, and audio can be fused in ways that remain interpretable, robust, and usable outside benchmark conditions.

I care about the full stack: model ideas, evaluation utilities, demo interfaces, and product-facing workflows. The goal is not only to publish, but to make research legible to collaborators and useful in practice.

Working style

  • Research questions grounded in real noisy data
  • Fast prototype loops from idea to visible demo
  • Strong preference for systems that can be inspected and reused
