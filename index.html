About Me

I'm Zhishuo Zhao, a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Sichuan University. My research focuses on multimodal learning, affective computing, and robust speech understanding under real-world conditions. Specifically, I work on multimodal sentiment analysis, cross-modal contrastive optimization, and noise-resilient speech recognition. I aim to build interpretable and generalizable models that bridge language, vision, and audio for emotionally-aware AI systems.
